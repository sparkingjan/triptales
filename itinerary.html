Sonamarg Alpine Escape - 4 Days

Budget: INR 24,000 - 31,000 Type: Adventure + Family

Overview

Experience Sonamarg's glacier viewpoints, Sindh river valleys, and high-altitude meadows in a carefully paced route ideal for both active travelers and families. This itinerary balances scenic drives, short guided walks, local Kashmiri food stops, and comfortable overnight stays.

Day-wise Plan

Day 1: Srinagar Arrival & Sonamarg Transfer

  • Pickup from Srinagar airport and scenic drive to Sonamarg.
  • Check-in at riverside stay and evening acclimatization walk.
  • Dinner featuring local wazwan-inspired dishes.

Day 2: Thajiwas Glacier Excursion

  • Morning pony-assisted route to Thajiwas glacier viewpoint.
  • Snow-point activities and guided photography stops.
  • Return by sunset and bonfire session at hotel lawn.

Day 3: Gagangir Valley & Local Market

  • Breakfast drive to Gagangir valley for short meadow trek.
  • Lunch at family-run cafe with trout special menu.
  • Evening market visit for saffron, dry fruits, and woolens.

Day 4: Return to Srinagar

  • Relaxed breakfast and final mountain viewpoint stop.
  • Transfer to Srinagar with optional Dal Lake drop-off.
  • Trip wrap-up and departure assistance.
